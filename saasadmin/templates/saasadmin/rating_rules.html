How it works

Every client starts at the Starting score. For each action, the engine multiplies its points by the number of occurrences found across all tenants reporting the client — loans, repayment transactions, and the full profile history (identity changes and past default/recovery episodes are counted even after they are cleared). The Cap limits the total points one factor can contribute. The final score is clamped to 0–1000 and its /10 value (0–100) is written back to every matched client profile.

Changes apply on the next recompute: nightly for the whole database, or immediately for a client whenever a tenant pays to view their report.
