How resolution works

Auto-linked clusters share a hard identifier (NID / passport / licence) or full name + date of birth — the rating engine already treats them as one person; confirming just records the decision. Needs review groups share a name but the matcher could not prove they are one person (missing or different DOB, no shared ID) — only you can decide.

Three outcomes: Merge keeps one profile and folds everything into it (for true duplicates, ideally same tenant); Same person keeps one profile per tenant but confirms a single shared rating; Different people permanently stops the matcher from ever merging them.
