Why Record Consent?

PNG's credit bureau framework requires lenders to hold documented consent before sharing borrower data with DCC or third parties.

Consent records are stored per client and linked to your organisation. They are viewable in the borrower's credit file audit trail.

Credit Check Authorization — required before running a credit check.
Data Sharing — required before uploading to DCC.
Third-Party Access — required before a partner accesses via the API.
