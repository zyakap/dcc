Our Services

We provide a range of services to help members make informed credit and business decisions. Here are the main services DCC offers, along with an explanation of each:

Consumer Credit Reporting

Detailed reports on the credit history of individual consumers. By accessing these reports, lenders can assess the creditworthiness of potential borrowers, helping them make informed lending decisions and manage risk more effectively

Business Credit Reporting

provides insights into the credit history of businesses. It helps companies evaluate the financial stability of potential business partners or customers, reducing the risk of extending credit to unreliable entities and ensuring prompt payments

Default Management

allows members to upload and manage debts that are in default. It helps in tracking and recording unpaid debts beyond the agreed credit terms, aiding in debt recovery processes and encouraging timely payments

Debt Collection Assistance

Assist members with their debt collection efforts. Strategies for pursuing unpaid debts, negotiating settlements, and legal actions if necessary. Effective debt collection services help improve cash flow and reduce bad debt losses for businesses.

Monitoring and Alerts

Members can place monitors on debtor accounts to receive notifications when there are updates or new information. This proactive service helps businesses stay informed about changes in the credit status of their debtors, enabling timely actions to mitigate risks.

Credit Approval Processes

DCC helps streamline the credit approval process for its members by providing tools and data to evaluate credit applications quickly and accurately. This includes automated checks and balances that ensure compliance with internal policies and regulatory requirements.

Data Security and Confidentiality

Ensures the security and confidentiality of data. DCC implements robust security measures, similar to those used in e-banking, to protect sensitive information. This includes secure access controls, encryption, and comprehensive audit trails to monitor data access and usage.

Compliance and Accuracy Assurance

Maintaining the accuracy of credit data is essential for the reliability of the services provided. DCC has stringent quality control processes to verify the information it receives and stores. Additionally, a compliance committee oversees operations to ensure adherence to legal and ethical standards.
